--- Hints 

Many autoshops are or become scams.


Many scam by selling the same material to many customers, or by using
it just after the check done prior to the selling.

Any shop named after a serious one, or resembling it, may be a scam.
For example if something.su is OK then something.com may not be so.

Be aware that many sites do scam selectively.

Beware, many crooks create an autoshop to scam then promote it on forums.

To obtain a sublist of a given type, for example all "OK" sites, just use "grep" on Unix or "search" on Windows. Example (Unix): grep OK CCSHOPROJ--shops_CC_bank.txt |grep -v maybe

Some markets need a deposit or an invitation.  For some invitations are sold on Evolution.


2015: Vault Market CCs' quality is gliding down.


On some markets one cannot buy using a Tor exit node, you have to access to
the shop through a proxy (note that you can register and send BTC to
your account).  This is for the card seller to check that you have one,
because carding throough Tor is a good way to burn the CC.

Good merchants: Yasuo and Redson, verify their PGP keys.
